Special characters

Feature Purpose Example
Small っ (tsu) Indicates a pause or doubled consonant ちょっと (cho-tto)
Long vowel ー Extends the preceding vowel sound コーヒー (ko-o-hi-i)
Small ゃ, ゅ, ょ Combines sounds into one syllable きゃ (kya), しゅ (shu)
Nasal ん (n) Creates a nasal sound or pause にほん (ni-ho-n)

Polite Phrases & Apologies

Japanese Romaji English

どういたしまして Dou itashimashite. You’re welcome.

Japan’s Prefectures

Japan is divided into 47 prefectures, known in Japanese as:

都道府県（と・どう・ふ・けん）

Each refers to a type of prefecture:

  • 都（と） = to → Only for Tokyo
  • 道（どう） = dou → Only for Hokkaidō
  • 府（ふ） = fu → Osaka & Kyoto
  • 県（けん） = ken → All other 43 prefectures
